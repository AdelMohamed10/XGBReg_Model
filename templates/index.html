House Prices Prediction App

Discover how machine learning can predict house prices based on various features of properties and neighborhoods.

Problem Definition

Predicting house prices is a crucial aspect of the real estate industry. Accurate predictions help stakeholders like buyers, sellers, and investors to make informed decisions. Our model aims to predict the Median House Value for properties based on several factors, such as geographical location, demographics, and property attributes.

By analyzing historical data, our machine learning model can provide fair and reliable predictions to assist in real estate decision-making.

About the Dataset

The dataset used for this application includes information about housing data from various regions. It contains the following features that influence the price of a house:

Feature Description
longitude Geographical longitude of the property location.
latitude Geographical latitude of the property location.
housingMedianAge The median age of the houses in the neighborhood.
totalRooms Total number of rooms in the property.
totalBedrooms Total number of bedrooms in the property.
population The total population of the area where the property is located.
households Number of households in the neighborhood.
medianIncome The median income of households in the area (in tens of thousands of dollars).
medianHouseValue The median value of houses in the area (target variable).
